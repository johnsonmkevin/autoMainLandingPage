Who we are

At Autograf Motor AB in Härnösand, we believe buying a car should be an easy and exciting experience. We offer a hand-picked selection of newer Swedish-sold cars, but our expertise doesn't stop at the border. Dreaming of a classic from North America? We import American legends and offer a full "turn-key" import service for those who have found a vehicle in the USA or Canada. We handle the paperwork, the shipping, and the logistics, delivering the car directly to your home. With our help obtaining Chamber of Commerce certificates and securing the best possible financing, all you have to do is enjoy the ride.
